📌 Placement Pilot

From Guesswork to Great Work

Placement Pilot is a platform designed to simplify campus placement preparation by providing real interview experiences, smart filtering tools, and peer interaction.

🚀 Features
📊 Verified Interview Experiences
Real questions and insights shared by students
🔍 Smart Company Search
Filter by company, role, and difficulty
✅ Eligibility Checker
Find companies based on your profile
📈 Personalized Dashboard
Track your preparation and progress
💬 PrepTalk (Peer Chat)
Ask doubts and get help in real-time
🧠 Problem It Solves

Students often rely on scattered information and random advice.
Placement Pilot brings everything into one structured and reliable platform.
